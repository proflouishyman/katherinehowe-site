Author

Katherine Howe

Katherine Howe is a New York Times bestselling and award-winning historian and novelist, and co-author with Anderson Cooper of the #1 New York Times bestsellers Vanderbilt and Astor.

Her newest novel is A True Account: Hannah Masury's Sojourn Amongst the Pyrates, Written by Herself.
